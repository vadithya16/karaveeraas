About Us

Karaveera Accounting Services and Karaveera Seva Kendra is located at B.C. Road, near Somayaji hospital, in Brahmana Parishath Building. We provide wide range of services in the areas of accounting, taxation, business consultancy and various common services needed for the local people. We aim at delivering quality services at an affordable price in timely basis. We are a team having post graduates and graduates in commerce under the able supervision of well experienced Chartered Accountant.

We offer various services relating to Accounting, Taxation and Business Consultancy. We provide end-to-end solutions for your business ideas. We are experienced in the field of Book keeping, various types of audits, preparation of project reports for PMFME, PMEGP, bank loans etc., Income tax return filing, GST filing etc.
